Homework for lesson 7

1) (2 points)

The small ball rotates inside the cone without friction.

The trajectory is closed. The highest and lowest heights of trajectory are $h_1$ and $h_0$.

Find velocities $v_1$ and $v_0$ at these points.

Remark: use the conservation of energy and angular momentum laws.

Answer: $v_0^2=\displaystyle\frac{2gh^2_1}{h_0+h_1}$ and $v_1^2=\displaystyle\frac{2gh^2_0}{h_0+h_1}.$

2) (1 point) Potential $\varphi(x,y,z)=xy^2+ye^z\sin z$. Find the force $\overrightarrow{F}(x,y,z).$

3) (1 point)

$\overrightarrow{F}(x,y,z)=\left(\begin{array}{r}-y\\x\\0\end{array}\right)$

doesn't admit potential.

4) (2 points) Prove that the force

$\overrightarrow{F}(x,y,z)=\left(\begin{array}{r}\sin y\\x\cos y\\z^2\end{array}\right)$

admits potential. Find it, i.e find $\varphi(x,y,z)$

5) (2 points) Prove that $\overrightarrow{F}=-k\overrightarrow{r}$ admits potential. Find it.
