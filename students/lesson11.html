Homework for lesson 11

1)(2 points)

Car moves in Luxembourg $\varphi=49^\circ$ from north to south with velocity $v=100km/h$.

Mass of the car $m=1000 kg$. Radius of the Earth $R=6400 km$

 a) Find total inertia force $\overrightarrow{F}_{inertia}$ that acts to the car (in the reference frame of the Earth)

 b) Find $\displaystyle\frac{mg-P}{mg}$, where $P$ - is a weight of the car.

2)(2 points)

Thin tube of length $l$ rotates in horizontal plane with constant angular velocity $\omega$ along the point $O$.

Small ball $m$ initially is set at the middle of the tube and has initial velocity zero.

At what time $t$ the ball will reach the corner of the tube?
