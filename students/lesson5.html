Homework for lesson 5

1) (2 points)

$k_1, k_2$ - spring constants.

$l_1, l_2$ - lengths of unstretched springs $(l_1+l_2\neq l)$

Suppose that $v_0$ is sufficiently small such that the body will never reach the walls during the motion.

Suppose that there is no friction.

  a) Find an equilibrium point.

  b) Find $x(t)$ if at $t=0$ body is at equilibrium point and $v(0)=v_0$

2) (1 point) Find all possible solutions of equation

$\ddot{x}-6\dot{x}+13x=13$

3) (1 point) Find all possible solutions of equation

$\ddot{x}-6\dot{x}=12$

By the way you can check whether your solution of differential equation is correct or not here: WolframAlpha.

4) (2 points)

Somebody puts small ball $m$ inside the glass of liquid.

The body starts to fall down $(\rho_{ball}>\rho_{liq}).$

Find $x(t)$ if $x(0)=0$ and $v(0)=0$.

Suppose that liquid friction force $\overrightarrow{F_{fr}}=-\alpha\overrightarrow{v}$

Remark: don't forget about Archimedes force!

$\displaystyle\overrigharrow{F}_{arch}=-\rho_{liq}\overrightarrow{g}V=-\frac{\rho_{liq}}{\rho_{ball}}m\overrightarrow{g}$.
