Homework for lesson 12. (Bring it to my office G112 or send it by email before your exam.)

1)(2 points)

The cart moves with constant acceleratrion $\overrightarrow{a}$ that is directed to the right-hand side. The angle between stick and the floor is constant and equals to $\varphi.$

Left wall of the cart is rough and at the point A coefficient of friction is equal to $\mu$. The floor is smooth and at point B there is no friction.

Find the minimum acceleration of the cart $a$ s.t. the stick is stable and doesn't fall down.

Hint: Write equations $\sum\limits_i\overrightarrow{M}_i=0$ and $\sum\limits_i\overrightarrow{F}_i=0 $ in non inertial reference frame of the cart. Don't forget about inertial force $\overrightarrow{F}_{inertial}=-m\overrightarrow{a}$ that like $m\overrightarrow{g}$ acts on the center of the stick.

2)(2 points)

Write lagrangian of the body $m$ that attached to the spring $(k,l_0)$ and oscillates along vertical axis $OY$ (1-dimensional movement). From lagrangian find equations of the motion and solve them.
