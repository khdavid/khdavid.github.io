Homework for lesson 4

1) (3 points)

Disc $C\in x0y$ with radius $r=1.$

$\overrightarrow{v}(x,y,z)=(y,-x,x^4+y^7).$

Compute:

 a) $\displaystyle\oint\limits_{\partial C}\overrightarrow{v}\cdot d\overrightarrow{r}.$

 b) $\displaystyle\int\limits_{C}\mathrm{rot}\overrightarrow{v}\cdot d\overrightarrow{S}.$

2) (2 points) In polar coordinates: $\varphi(t)=\omega t+\varepsilon\cos t, r(t)=a+b\sin t.$

Compute the velocity $\overrightarrow{v}(t)$ and the acceleration $\overrightarrow{a}(t)$

3) (4 points)

Satellite rotates around the Earth along the circular orbit.

The radius of the orbit $r=15\cdot 10^{3} km$, mass of the Earth $M_E=6\cdot 10^{24} kg$.

The gravitational constant $\displaystyle G= 6.7\cdot 10^{-11} \frac{m^3}{kg\cdot s^2}.$

  a) Find the acceleration $\overrightarrow{a}(t)$ of the satellite.

  b) Find the velocity $\overrightarrow{v}(t)$ of the satellite.

  c) Find the period of rotation $T$.

  d) Find the radius of the geostationary orbit (the period of the satellite's rotation

equals to the period of Earth's rotation $T_E=24h$)
