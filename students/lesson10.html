Homework for lesson 10

1)(2 points)

Small ball rotates inside the circle tube with constant velocity $v$ (in the reference frame of the tube.)

The tube rotates along vertical axis with angular velocity $\omega=\omega_0\sin(\beta t^2),\quad \omega_0,\beta=\mathrm{const}.$

The angle between the tube and the vertical axis is $\theta$. Find instantaneous velocity and acceleration of the ball $v^{(lab)}$ and $a^{(lab)}$

at the point $P$ at time $\displaystyle t=\sqrt{\frac{\pi}{3\beta}}$ (in laboratory reference frame.)

2)(2 points)

The straight tube rotates along vertical axis with constant angular velocity $\omega$.

At the point $P$ are given velocity $v$ and acceleration $a$ of small ball (in the reference frame of the tube.)

 a)Find instantaneous velocity of the ball $v^{(lab)}$ at the point $P$ (in laboratory reference frame.)

 b)Find instantaneous acceleration of the ball $a^{(lab)}$ at the point $P$ (in laboratory reference frame.)
